Our Mission

Redefining Indian Beauty Tech

Bee represents a vision of effortless luxury, connecting discerning guests with elite styling craftsmen.

The Startup Story

Founded in Bangalore in 2025, Bee emerged from a simple realization: booking high-end salons remained fragmented, despite top salons charging premium rates. High-society guests encountered double bookings, waiting queues, and inconsistent services.

We partnered with top luxury chains, hotels, and international hair directors to aggregate under a single unified platform. Driven by technology, styled by elegance.
